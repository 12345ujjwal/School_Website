About Us

Sun Shine Public School run by a social organization named "SARVODAYA JAN KALYAN SAMITI" which was formally inaugurated & laid its foundation stone on December 8, 2006 by Honorable Shri Kailash Singh Yadav Ji, (Industrial Minister, U.P. Government). The dream of the honorable Chairman of the Samiti Shri. S.N. Singh Ji, is harmonious development of all aspects of a child's personality to face the challenging world. Sun Shine is a premier institution located in a lush green surrounding covering 2.5 acres of land. Sun Shine Piblic School is away from the humdrum of life giving a perfect educational atmosphere, well connected with road and rail. Nearest railway station is only 7km from school campus. The school has permanent infrastructural building with a spacious playground spread in 1.5 acres of land, combined with modern teaching aid and carefully drawn faculty members from every corner of the country.
